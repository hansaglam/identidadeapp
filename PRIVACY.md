# Rito — Privacy Policy

> **Published version:** [`legal/privacy.html`](legal/privacy.html) → GitHub Pages. Edit the HTML file for store links.

**Rito: Habit & Identity Journey** (TR: *Alışkanlık ve Kimlik Yolculuğu*)

Last updated: June 2026

## Summary

Rito helps you build habits through micro-actions, check-ins, and a 66-day identity journey. **Your data stays on your device by default.** We do not run a cloud account or sync service for your habit content.

## What we store on your device

- Profile (name, habit, anchor, preferences)
- Daily check-ins and automaticity ratings
- Mind notes and journey reflections
- Tomorrow plans and local backup exports
- Subscription status (via Apple/Google; not payment card data)

## What we do not collect

- We do not sell personal data.
- We do not upload Mind notes, check-ins, or habit content to our servers by default.
- Payment card details are handled only by Apple App Store or Google Play.

## Notifications

If you enable reminders, the app schedules local notifications on your device. You can turn them off in Profile → Notifications.

## Backups

JSON exports you create are **not encrypted** by the app. Store them in a safe place you control.

## Premium

Premium is billed through your app store account. Manage or cancel in your store subscription settings.

## Children

Rito is not directed at children under 13.
