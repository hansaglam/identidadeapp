## Description (EN)

Rito helps you build habits through micro-actions, not willpower marathons.

- One clear move on your Today card — 3 to 15 seconds
- Anchor your habit to an existing routine (Tiny Habits style)
- 66-day identity journey with phases, coach pack, and Mind journal
- Gentle recovery when you miss days — no shame, small restart

Premium unlocks the full 66-day map, Identity Mirror, SDT pulse, and unlimited Mind notes. Tomorrow plan and daily check-in stay free.
